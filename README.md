## ⚡ Everything you copy. At your fingertips.

OptionV runs quietly in your menubar. Whenever you copy text, images, or files, they are safely stored in your local clipboard history. When you need them, they are just a single keystroke away. 

No bloated features, no accounts to create, and no cloud sync—just pure, blazing-fast retrieval so you can stay in your creative flow.

Simply press `⌥ + V` anywhere on your Mac.

---

## ✨ Why OptionV?

### 🚀 Blazing Fast
Built natively for macOS to ensure instantaneous search and retrieval. Your workflow shouldn't pause while your tools catch up. OptionV is optimized to render thousands of history items in milliseconds.

### 🎨 Premium Aesthetics
We believe productivity tools should look as good as the work you create with them. OptionV features a stunning, ultra-modern dark-theme design with glassmorphic elements and electric gold accents. It feels like a premium, natural extension of macOS.

### 🔒 Privacy by Design
Everything you copy stays strictly on your device. We don't read your data, we don't sync it to external servers, and we don't sell it. Your passwords, API tokens, and private messages are exclusively yours.

### ⌨️ Keyboard-First Focus
Keep your hands right where they are. Instantly search, select, strip formatting, and paste using lightning-fast keyboard shortcuts designed specifically for power users.

## 🛠 Unmatched Capability

- **Universal Search:** Instantly scrub through your extensive history using intelligent search.
- **Smart Paste:** Strip rich text formatting on the fly, pasting pure text wherever you need it.
- **Rich Media Support:** Effortlessly preview, copy, and paste local files and images, not just text.
- **History Management:** Pin important, frequently used snippets and exclude sensitive applications (like Password Managers) from ever being logged.

---

## 📥 Quick Start

1. **Download** the latest version from our official website.
2. **Install** by moving `OptionV.app` to your `/Applications` folder.
3. **Launch** OptionV. Upon first run, macOS will ask you to grant Accessibility permissions so OptionV can safely monitor your clipboard changes.
4. **Activate** by pressing `⌥ + V` to open your clipboard history from anywhere.
